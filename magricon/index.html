The M.A. Consulting Group is a regional organisation providing consulting services in economics, finance and management, comprising of two companies; Mwaniki Associates Limited and Agriconsult Limited incorporated in Kenya in 1983 and 1984 respectively

Our Philosopy

The Group proceeds from the premise that development is better managed by local experts, supported where desirable by foreign professionals. This combination of resources and talent is essential in developing practical solutions to policy, institutional and business challenges. The Group utilises appropriately skilled, knowledgeable and experienced consultants and advisors who approach each assignment with a combination of rigorous and objective analysis, backed by practical experience and a keen understanding of the local context. In addition to our regional experience and a large pool of qualified and experienced associates, we maintain strong networks with reputable overseas partners in Europe, North America and Asia, who we regularly associate with in projects where overseas experience and specialization is required. In order to generate future expertise for the growth of the Group and add to the level of consulting expertise in Kenya and the East Africa Regions, young consultants are trained under the guidance of senior professional staff and associates.

Professional and Support Services

Our professional staff is comprised of a core group of in-house consultants who are available for an assignment depending upon the area of expertise needed. In addition, the Group counts among its associates over 250 professionals who are highly qualified in their areas of specialisation. The Group maintains excellent office space and support facilities, located in the heart of Kenya's capital city of Nairobi. The offices are in International House, across the street from the Nairobi Hilton International Hotel. Support facilities comprise of secretarial service, office space, and excellent data-processing and communication equipment with Internet connection.
